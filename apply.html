Apply to Join the Backrooms Group

If you're interested in joining the Backrooms group, please read through the duties and expectations below before submitting your application.

Duties:

  • 📋 Fill out weekly reports on your experiences in the Backrooms
  • 🔦 Explore uncharted areas of the Backrooms and document your findings
  • 🛠️ Help repair damaged walls and floors in the Backrooms
  • 👻 Communicate with and befriend the entities that reside in the Backrooms
  • 🍔 Make sure the vending machines are fully stocked with snacks and drinks
  • 🧹 Sweep up any debris or trash found in the Backrooms
  • 🚪 Open any doors that lead to new areas of the Backrooms
  • 🔑 Keep track of all the different keys found in the Backrooms and their corresponding doors
  • 🎨 Decorate the walls with murals and paintings to brighten up the Backrooms

Expectations:

  • 👀 Always be aware of your surroundings and stay alert for any dangers
  • 👥 Work together with other members of the Backrooms group to achieve common goals
  • 💬 Communicate clearly and effectively with other members of the group
  • 📅 Complete all assigned duties on time and to the best of your abilities
  • 🔒 Keep any classified information about the Backrooms group strictly confidential
  • 👍 Show respect and kindness to all entities encountered in the Backrooms
  • 🤝 Build positive relationships with other entities in the Backrooms to ensure peaceful coexistence
