The Smilers

The Smilers are humanoid entities that possess a perpetual grin, appearing harmless at first glance. However, they are known to emit a high-pitched, deafening screech that can cause severe auditory damage and disorientation.

  • High Risk Do not approach or make eye contact.
  • High Risk If encountered, immediately plug your ears and retreat as quickly and quietly as possible.
  • High Risk If cornered or unable to retreat, use a high-decibel noise or distraction to incapacitate the entity and escape.

The Skin-Stealers

High Risk

These entities are capable of infiltrating groups by assuming the identity of someone within the group. They then attack from within, utilizing the element of surprise. Avoid trusting new additions to your group and watch for any strange behavior or inconsistencies in behavior.

The Hounds

Risk Level: Medium-High

These entities resemble large, aggressive dogs and hunt in packs. They are extremely fast and difficult to outrun. It is recommended to have a reliable light source and move cautiously through any areas known to be inhabited by them. Avoid eye contact and remain still if one is encountered.

The Faceless

Risk Level: Medium

Not to be confused with the faceless gang, These entities are humanoid in shape but lack a distinct facial structure. They are generally non-aggressive but can become hostile if provoked. It is recommended to avoid confrontation and give them a wide berth.

The Crawlers

Risk Level: Low-Medium

These entities move on all fours and are generally non-aggressive. They are often found in groups and can be easily avoided by moving quietly and maintaining a safe distance.

The Watchers

Risk Level: Low

These entities appear as stationary, faceless humanoids that silently observe wanderers. They do not move or react to stimuli and are generally harmless. However, it is recommended to maintain a safe distance and avoid prolonged exposure to them.
